## Core Principles for All Content

1. **Reject Pathology, Embrace Neurodiversity**
   - Never use "disorder" without reframing it
   - Always validate the person first, challenge the system second
   - Frame differences as features, not bugs

2. **Science-Backed, Not Feelings-Based**
   - Ground every claim in neurobiology
   - Use concrete metaphors (race car, energy vampires, electrical grid)
   - Make the abstract tangible

3. **Radical Acceptance + Strategic Action**
   - Validate the struggle
   - Explain the mechanism
   - Provide the action plan

4. **Bold, Direct, No Bullshit**
   - Strategic profanity for emphasis
   - Direct address ("You know that feeling...")
   - Provocative questions that land

5. **Always End with Action**
   - Never just insight
   - Always "What now?"
   - Concrete, achievable next steps
